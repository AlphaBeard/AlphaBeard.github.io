About Me

Hi, my name is Kyle! I recently graduated from UCSC with a B.S. in Robotics Engineering Student where I completed my senior thesis on bio-inspired robotic systems using Myriapoda biology. In my time at UCSC, I was a Tau Beta Pi Engineering Society member, the Lead Hardware Engineer of the on-campus startup Yetkasonics, and a hardware consultant for the on-campus startup AgTech.

I have considerable experience with low-level embedded systems using the PIC32 microcontroller, the PSoC 6 microcontroller, and retail microprocessors like the Raspberry Pi and Arduino. In addition, I have experience with Basys 3 FPGA board. My programming experience includes excellent knowledge of C and proficient working knowledge of Object-Oriented Languages. I taught myself web development paradigms and familiarized myself with NodeJS, React, Javascript, Sass, and MongoDB through developing several web apps shown above. I currently work at FS Studio as a Tele-Ops Operator.
